What is Express Entry?

Express Entry is a system used by the Canadian government to manage applications for permanent residence from skilled workers. It covers three main programs:

  • Federal Skilled Worker Program: For skilled workers with foreign work experience.
  • Federal Skilled Trades Program: For skilled workers in specific trades.
  • Canadian Experience Class: For skilled workers who have Canadian work experience.

How to Improve Your CRS Score?

If your score is lower than the recent cut-offs, consider these strategies:

  • Improve Language Scores: Retaking IELTS or CELPIP to reach CLB 9 or 10 can significantly boost your score.
  • Provincial Nominee Program (PNP): A nomination from a province gives you an automatic 600 points.
  • Gain Work Experience: Additional years of skilled work experience, especially in Canada, will add points.
  • Higher Education: Completing a Master's or PhD can provide more points for human capital.
